A Comfortable Stay Experience

The Standard Room is thoughtfully designed to provide our guests with comfort and tranquility during their stay. With an area of 25 square meters, the room offers enough space to relax and unwind.

The room features an elegant decor that combines authenticity with modernity, with calm colors that create an atmosphere of relaxation and peace.

  • Flat-screen TV with a variety of channels
  • Independent air conditioning and free Wi-Fi service

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about the Standard Room

Can an extra bed be added to the Standard Room?

Yes, an extra bed for a child can be added to the Standard Room for an additional fee. Please indicate this in your reservation request.

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in time is from 2:00 PM, and check-out time is until 12:00 PM. Early check-in or late check-out can be arranged based on availability.

Are pets allowed?

We do not currently accommodate pets in the hotel, with the exception of trained service animals.

Is there a cancellation policy for the reservation?

You can cancel for free up to 48 hours before the arrival date. After that, a cancellation fee may apply.
